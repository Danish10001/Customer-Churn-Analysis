## Customer Churn Analysis

Analyzing customer data to identify factors influencing customer churn. The analysis focuses on contract type, payment methods, tenure and service usage to understand patterns that lead to customer churn.

### Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib / Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

### Key Insights

* Customers on month-to-month contracts show significantly higher churn compared to long-term contracts.
* Electronic check users have higher churn rates than other payment methods.
* Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn, highlighting the importance of early engagement.
